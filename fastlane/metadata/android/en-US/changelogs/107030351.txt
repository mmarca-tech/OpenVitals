OpenVitals is now built on Flutter: same app, same features, same Health Connect data, and the foundation for an iOS version later. Your settings carry over automatically - goals, custom drinks, the caffeine profile, paired sensors and reminders are all migrated on first launch, and your Health Connect data is untouched. Only the dashboard tile order and quick-beverage widget selection need setting again. Apple Health imports are much faster and now show real progress while scanning.
