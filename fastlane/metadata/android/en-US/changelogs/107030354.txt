A fix release. The steps, distance, calories and elevation your watch records on a walk or run now appear: the watch writes them separately from the activity, so its page said "Not available" while charting that same activity's cadence and splits. Speed and cadence charts are back, and the metrics list no longer advertises figures an activity never had. Also fixes the empty gap on the recording screen, the crash when importing large files, and widgets opening the wrong picker.
