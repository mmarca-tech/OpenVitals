Indoor activities can be imported at last. A treadmill run, a trainer ride, a strength session - files with no GPS - were refused as broken. They were not broken: they had no route, which is not the same thing. The app now also reads TCX, the format Strava and Garmin export indoor work as. Import a whole folder of FIT files at once. An indoor ride no longer imports as a run. And activities now show an elevation profile, plus a speed graph even when the device recorded no speed.
