The app's insides were rebuilt, and the rebuild is what found the bugs. The sleep card called your watch's recordings hand-typed, and never counted the nights you really did type in. The hydration goal bar filled up from a single logged day. A day's heart rate could print an average outside its own low and high. The skin-temperature card went blank while its chart kept drawing. Respiratory rate showed two averages at once. And a screen could spin forever after a failed permission check.
