OpenVitals is a privacy-first Health Connect dashboard and manual entry app for Android. It reads your health data on-device and lets you explicitly add supported manual metrics back into Health Connect. There is no account, no cloud sync, no ads, and no analytics.

Features:

* Daily dashboard with cards for activity, recovery, intake, body, heart, vitals, mindfulness, records, and optional cycle data
* Separate Add entry area for hydration, body measurements, vitals, and mindfulness sessions
* Period-based detail screens with Day / Week / Month / Year navigation
* Health Connect onboarding flow with availability checks and categorized read permissions
* Write permissions requested only when you add a supported manual entry
* Pull-to-refresh, range selection, period navigation, and calendar date picking
* Works without an internet connection
