OpenVitals is a privacy-first Health Connect dashboard for Android. It reads your health data entirely on-device — no account, no cloud sync, no ads, no analytics. The app is read-only and never writes health data back.

Features:

* Daily dashboard with cards for steps, distance, calories, hydration, workout, sleep, heart rate, and body metrics
* Period-based detail screens with Day / Week / Month / Year navigation
* Dedicated screens for Activity, Activities, Sleep, Heart rate, Body composition, and more
* Health Connect onboarding flow with availability checks and phased permission requests
* Pull-to-refresh, range selection, period navigation, and calendar date picking
* Works without an internet connection
