# Thanks

OpenVitals is an independent project, but some parts of the manual-entry work were shaped by existing open-source health and mindfulness apps.

## Inspiration

- [HydroTracker](https://github.com/Econ01/HydroTracker) inspired the hydration entry flow, especially the idea of quick drink and container choices. OpenVitals stores only the calculated hydration volume in Health Connect.
- [DailySitting](https://github.com/stigmergic-org/DailySitting) inspired the mindfulness timer flow, including configurable sitting duration, interval bells, and the save-or-discard session pattern.
