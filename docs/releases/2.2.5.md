# OpenVitals 2.2.5

Released 2026-07-14.

The download now says which phone it is for.

### Changed

- **The APKs are named after the architecture they are for.** 2.2.4 split the download in two, one per architecture, but named them after their internal version code — `OpenVitals-v2.2.4-1070303662.apk` — which tells you nothing about which one your phone wants. They are now `OpenVitals-v2.2.5-arm64-v8a.apk` and `OpenVitals-v2.2.5-armeabi-v7a.apk`. Take the arm64-v8a one unless you know your phone is an older 32-bit device; F-Droid and the Play Store still pick for you.

Nothing else changed. There is no behavioural difference between 2.2.4 and 2.2.5, and the app inside each APK is byte-for-byte what 2.2.4 shipped.
